THE TERMINAL

Behind the Grid: Craig Dominic

I’ve been a "tinkerer" since I could hold a screwdriver. What started as a childhood obsession with taking apart electronics and building custom desktops evolved into a lifelong mission to understand how the world stays connected.

As an adult, I took that curiosity into the telecommunications and electrical services field. My days are spent on the front lines of infrastructure—working with tower structures, fiber optics, and the physical backbone of the internet. If there’s a cable running into your house or a signal hitting your phone, I’ve likely worked on the systems that put it there.

Radio Frequency (RF) Expert Network Infrastructure Hardware Engineering Cybersecurity Enthusiast

My deepest expertise lies in Radio Frequency (RF) and signal propagation. Whether it’s optimizing a wireless bridge or analyzing spectrum interference, I thrive in the "invisible" layer of tech. I’ve spent years providing tech support and engineering solutions for complex systems that most people take for granted.

Craiglist.shop is where I bridge the gap between hobbyist passion and professional-grade execution. I review the gear I actually use in the field—hardware that is built for performance, security, and reliability.
